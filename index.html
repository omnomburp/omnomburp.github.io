How to use the course

Treat each page like a short lesson, not a static article

1. Read the mental model first

Start with the short conceptual framing so the symbols in the rest of the page have a job to do.

2. Walk the steps in order

Each chapter breaks the topic into numbered moves so you are not decoding the whole pipeline at once.

3. Use the lab as a question machine

Change one control, predict the result, then compare your prediction to the picture.

Good study question

What data entered the pipeline, what transformation happened, and why did the image change?

What to avoid

Do not memorize formulas before you know what visual problem each formula is solving.

Best first path

Read Chapters 1 through 7 in order on your first pass, then use Chapters 8 through 11 as the second half of the course.
